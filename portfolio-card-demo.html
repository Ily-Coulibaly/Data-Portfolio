Bike Share User Behavior Analysis

Comprehensive analysis of two years of hourly bike share usage data

This report analyzes two years of hourly bike share usage data, revealing actionable insights into customer behavior patterns between casual and registered users across weather conditions and seasonal variations.

Key Findings

• Registered users dominate weekdays (193 avg rides), casual users peak on weekends
• Temperature increases drive 6.2x more casual rides per degree vs 2.5x for registered
• Weather significantly impacts usage: clear/cloudy conditions outperform rainy/snowy by 40%
